The Geant4 benchmark app (PrimaryGeneratorAction) optionally uses EcoMug for
one of its source modes. To build that mode, download EcoMug.h yourself from
the official repository and place it in this directory:

  https://github.com/dr4kan/EcoMug

Reference: D. Pagano et al., "EcoMug: an Efficient COsmic MUon Generator",
Nucl. Instrum. Meth. A 1014 (2021) 165732. doi:10.1016/j.nima.2021.165732
